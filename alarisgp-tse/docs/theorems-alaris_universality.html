Theorems: alaris_universality

Universality theorems.
The universality template is: \forall s1, s2 in S: transit(s1, s2) & universal(s1) => universal(s2)
where universal(s) := filter1(s) = filter2(s)
Author:
  • Michael Harrison and Paolo Masci (QMUL, UMinho Braga and Newcastle University)
