Theorems: alaris_visibility

Visibility theorems.
The visibility template is: \forall s1, s2 in S: transit(s1, s2) & visible(s1) => visible(s2)
where visible(s) := filter(s) = p_filter_p(s)
Author:
  • Michael Harrison and Paolo Masci (QMUL, UMinho Braga and Newcastle University)
