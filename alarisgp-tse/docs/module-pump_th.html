Module: pump theory

Theory associated with the generic pump underlying several infusion pump interfaces. Maximum infusion rate, maximum and minimum vtbi, timeouts, battery maximum and minimums are parameterised. Further constraints minimum infusion must be less than or equal to maximum infusion. Maximum time must exceed time out. Maximum battery time plus one must be less than maximum time. Battery time minimum must be less than battery time maximum.
